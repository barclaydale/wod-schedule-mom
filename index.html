Rowing 0.0 Today we're going to follow a Chris Hinshaw video rowing at a moderate pace for reps of 75 seconds. The warm up is intended to find your sprint pace. Your moderate pace is half of your sprint. You will be doing 3 sprints. Record average wattage of each sprint, average the 3 wattages, and divide by 2. This is your moderate pace.

In the video they will do:
Warm Up: 5 x (30s easy pace, 20s Rest, 10s sprint, record average wattage)
Workout: 5 sets of (4 x (75s Row at moderate pace, 20s Rest)), Rest 2 min between sets

You will do:
Warm Up: Let the video run. Just watch during the 1st set, do the next 3 sets, and calculate moderate pace during 5th set.
Workout: Do at least the first 3 sets of the workout (43:48 in the video). If you feel good, follow along for the final two sets of the workout.
